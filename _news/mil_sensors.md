["Designing an Optimal Sensor Network via Minimizing Information Loss"](arxiv.org/abs/2512.05940) (joint work with Fernando Llorente, Katia Lamer, and Petar Djurić) is now accepted by Bayesian Analysis and available on the arXiv! We introduce a novel experimental design framework to leverage varational inference and digital twins in modern sensor placement tasks.
