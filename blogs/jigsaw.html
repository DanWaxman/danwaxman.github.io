Jigsaw Puzzles

This year for math club, we decided to hold all of our events online. I miss the social interaction, but one awesome part about this is the breakdown of geographical barriers --- it doesn't matter if someone lives in Canada, the Zoom call will be all the same. This is exactly what happened with George Hart, mathematical sculptor and overall legend. He gave a wonderful and interactive talk about warped jigsaw puzzles.

All of Dr. Hart's talk happened on square lattices which were later transformed, and my friend Kurt asked if he had any results on other tilings of the plane, e.g. tilings of equilateral triangles, which he hadn't. So Kurt and I decided to spend some time messing around with these. I wrote this page for some interactive painting tools for this, and to update as we want more.
